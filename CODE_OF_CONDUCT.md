# Code of Conduct

hoolah is a small language project. Please keep participation kind, precise,
and welcoming.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful of Filipino language variation and dialect boundaries.
- Give citations when discussing word validity.
- Critique code and data, not people.
- Assume good faith, and be willing to revise when shown better evidence.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, slurs, unwanted
sexual attention, and repeated disruption of issues or pull requests.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who make
the project unsafe or unworkable for others.
